Hi! I'm Andrew Caldwell, a Research Scientist at UC San Diego working on systems biology of Alzheimer's Disease.

I'm interested in experimental methods for high throughput sequencing, bioinformatics data processing analysis, -omics integration, and mechanistic systems biology of neurodegenerative diseases. My recent work on this topic can be found in Science Advances and Alzheimer's Research & Therapy.

You can find me posting on Bluesky and Twitter. I'm also a big fan of coffee. You can find my coffee-related musings on Instagram @CaldwellCoffee.
